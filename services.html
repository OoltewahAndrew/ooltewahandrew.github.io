Services

Clear scopes. Practical deliverables. No jargon tax.

Staffing & Coverage Analysis

Problem it solves: Unclear staffing needs, overtime pressure, or questions about coverage adequacy.

What you get:

  • Staffing curves by hour/day (where coverage is thin or strong)
  • Coverage gaps tied to call demand and response patterns
  • Plain-language findings and recommended options
Typical timeline: 2–3 weeks Board-ready report

Call Volume & Demand Modeling

Problem it solves: Decisions based on totals instead of demand patterns.

What you get:

  • Call volume by time, unit type, and response category
  • Demand overlays that show peak pressure windows
  • Visuals designed for boards and committees
Typical timeline: 2–4 weeks RMS/CAD-agnostic

Budget & Millage Support

Problem it solves: Difficulty connecting operational needs to funding requests.

What you get:

  • Cost drivers tied directly to service demand and coverage needs
  • Scenario comparisons (status quo vs proposed)
  • Materials designed for public and board review
Typical timeline: 3–4 weeks Meeting-ready slides

Executive Dashboards & Reports

Problem it solves: Data exists but isn’t decision-ready.

What you get:

  • Executive dashboards focused on operational and budget questions
  • Plain-language summaries for non-technical audiences
  • Exportable reports for packets and committee review
Typical timeline: 2–3 weeks PDF + visuals

Custom Board Presentations

Problem it solves: Technical findings that don’t land with elected officials.

What you get:

  • Board-ready slide decks with simplified visuals
  • Talking points for chiefs and administrators
  • Clear framing for public meetings and stakeholder questions
Typical timeline: 1–2 weeks after analysis Public-meeting safe
Example board question we design for: “What changed, what does it cost, and what do we get for it?”
