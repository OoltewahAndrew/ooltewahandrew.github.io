Who We Serve

Governance-aware, operations-literate analysis

Townships & Villages

We understand board dynamics, budget constraints, and the need for defensible, plain-language reporting. Outputs are designed for packets, committees, and public meetings.

  • Budget cycle support and scenario comparisons
  • Clarity for trustees and administrators
  • Documentation that holds up under scrutiny

Fire Departments

Paid, paid-on-call, and combination departments. We focus on staffing, coverage, and operational demand without requiring new platforms.

  • Staffing curves and peak demand windows
  • Workload and response pattern analysis
  • Board-ready visuals

EMS / Fire-Based EMS

Demand modeling and coverage questions are different in EMS. We account for call volume, unit-hours, peak overlaps, and the real constraints your crews feel.

  • Unit utilization and call overlap patterns
  • Coverage impacts of scheduling changes
  • Plain-language recommendations

Joint Authorities & Mutual Aid Regions

When multiple entities share service delivery, governance matters. We build analysis that respects operational reality and shared decision-making.

  • Cross-agency comparisons (apples-to-apples)
  • Regional demand patterns and shared coverage stress
  • Outputs usable by multiple boards
